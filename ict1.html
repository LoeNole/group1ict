Senior High school Strand

Senior high school strands offer students a diverse range of educational tracks to choose from, including the Academic Track with strands like Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) focusing on business subjects, Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) emphasizing science and math, Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) covering social sciences and humanities, and General Academic Strand (GAS) providing a broad spectrum of subjects. The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track includes Information and Communications Technology (ICT) for IT skills, Home Economics (HE) for home management skills, Industrial Arts (IA) for technical skills, and Agri-Fishery Arts (AFA) for agricultural and aquaculture skills. The Sports Track offers specialized training in various sports disciplines, while the Arts and Design Track encompasses visual arts, performing arts, multimedia arts, fashion design, and other creative fields. Each strand equips students with specific knowledge and skills, preparing them either for further education in college or direct entry into the workforce, aligning with their career aspirations and interests.
